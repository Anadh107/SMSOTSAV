PARMISH VERMA

Parmish Verma is a renowned Punjabi singer, actor, lyricist, and filmmaker who has emerged as one of the most influential youth icons in the Punjabi music and film industry. Known for his high-energy performances, powerful voice, and deeply relatable lyrics, Parmish Verma has built a massive fan following across India and beyond.

He began his journey behind the camera as a music video director, where his creative vision and storytelling skills gained recognition. His rise to fame came with chart-topping hits like Gaal Ni Kadni, Le Chakk Main Aa Gaya, Shada, and Aam Jahe Munde.
